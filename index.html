FAQ
Frequently asked questions
How does the AI receptionist sound? ▼
Our AI uses the latest voice synthesis technology to sound natural and professional. You can customize the voice, tone, and personality to match your brand. Most callers won't realize they're talking to an AI.
Can I customize what the AI says? ▼
You have full control over the greeting message, the AI's personality, how it handles different types of calls, escalation rules, and more. You can configure everything from the dashboard settings.
What happens if the AI can't handle a call? ▼
You can set up escalation rules to transfer calls to a human when needed — for example, if a caller is upset or asks a complex question the AI can't answer. The AI will take a message and notify you immediately.
How long does setup take? ▼
Most businesses are up and running in under 5 minutes. Sign up, configure your AI settings, and forward your phone number. That's it. No technical expertise required.
Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll get full access to all features on your chosen plan during the trial period.
